Our Story

In August 2015, Jesse and Megan were both active on Coffee Meets Bagel. When Megan was sent Jesse’s profile, she saw a picture of him hugging a puppy and decided to accept the match. After a couple weeks of texting, Jesse and Megan had their first date riding down the coast of Santa Barbara on Jesse’s motorcycle. They both quickly realized that there was a definite spark between each other.

Three years, countless memories, and a furry addition to the family later, Jesse popped the question at Huntington Library Botanical Gardens and Megan said yes.
